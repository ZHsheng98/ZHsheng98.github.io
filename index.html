About Me

Hi I am Zihao. I am pursuing the Master degree in control engineering at School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Associate Prof. Xue Shibei and Prof. Li Dewei. I have received the B.S. degree in automation from Xi’an Jiao Tong University in 2019.

My research interest falls in area of intelligent transportation systems, autonomous driving, deep learning and data mining. Particularly, I am currently working on the modeling and prediction of traffic networks using graph convolution networks.
